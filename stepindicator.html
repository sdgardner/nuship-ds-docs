Step Indicator

Step indicators show progress through a sequence of steps in a multi-step flow. Two layouts are available — Dot for compact, decorative use and Labeled for clear, numbered steppers with descriptive titles. Each step has three states: completed, active, and upcoming.

Accessibility

  • ARIA: Use role="list" on the stepper and role="listitem" on each step. The active step should carry aria-current="step"; completed steps should have an accessible name like "Step 1, completed".
  • Keyboard: Completed steps should be focusable so users can revisit them via Tab + Enter. Active and upcoming steps stay non-interactive.
  • Don't rely on colour alone: Pair colour state with shape (filled circle, check icon, outline) so colour-blind users can still distinguish completed, active, and upcoming.
  • Contrast: Active blue (#0083BB) on white meets WCAG AA. Verify the upcoming gray (#4B5563) on white at 7:1. In dark mode use the inverse — light text on a dark surface.
