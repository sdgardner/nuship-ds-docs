Tooltip

Tooltips surface short, contextual help when a user hovers, focuses, or taps an element. They come in two layouts — Simple for single-line hints and Descriptive for richer header + body explanations — and can point in any of four directions to anchor onto their trigger.

Accessibility

  • ARIA: Set role="tooltip" on the popup and link the trigger with aria-describedby. Avoid putting interactive content inside the tooltip itself.
  • Keyboard: Tooltips must open on Tab focus, not just hover. Esc dismisses an open tooltip without moving focus.
  • Hover behaviour: Don't rely on hover alone — touch and keyboard users can't trigger it. Maintain the tooltip while the pointer is over either the trigger or the popup.
  • Contrast: Header text (#374151) and body text (#4B5563) on the light surface meet WCAG AA. In dark mode use the inverse — light text on a dark surface.
