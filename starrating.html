Star Rating

Star Rating is a five-star scale for displaying or capturing user satisfaction, review scores, or quality ratings. Each star can be empty, half-filled, or full, supporting fractional values like 3.5 out of 5.

Variants

Star states:
  • Empty — outline only with 2px stroke; counted as 0
  • Half — left half filled; counted as 0.5
  • Full — solid fill with stroke; counted as 1

Accessibility

  • ARIA: Wrap the rating in role="img" with an aria-label like "4.5 out of 5 stars" so screen readers announce the score, not five separate stars. For interactive ratings use role="radiogroup".
  • Keyboard: When interactive, support Arrow Left/Right to decrement/increment and Home / End to jump to 0 or max. Show focus outline on the active star.
  • Don't rely on color alone: Pair the rating with a numeric value or text label so users with low vision or color-blindness can still understand the score.
  • Touch targets: Each star must have at least a 24×24 hit area when interactive. Add invisible padding around the icon if needed.
